About ReWardBin

We aim to create a cleaner, greener future where waste disposal is not just a civic duty but a rewarding experience. By partnering with government bodies, NGOs, and local communities, we plan to scale RewardBin into a nationwide initiative that promotes sustainability and responsible waste management. Join us in making waste collection smarter, efficient, and rewarding—because a cleaner world starts with small actions!

How It Works Request Pickup – Households can schedule trash pickup with a single tap. Automated Matching – The nearest vendor gets notified and accepts the pickup request. Real-Time Tracking – Users can track the vendor’s arrival and completion of the task. Earn Rewards – Users receive points and coupons for responsible waste disposal. Vendor Incentives – Pickup vendors receive salaries and performance-based bonuses.
