Business Waste & Recycling FAQs

1 How can I report waste in my area?

Answer: You can report waste by clicking a photo and uploading it on our platform. Simply pin the location where the waste is located, and our team will schedule a pickup as soon as possible.

2. How does the on-demand waste pickup service work?

Answer: If you have waste that needs urgent disposal, you can request an on-demand pickup. Just select your preferred time, and our collection team will arrive at your location to collect and dispose of the waste responsibly.

3. What rewards do I get for reporting waste?

Answer: Every time you report waste or schedule a pickup, you earn eco-points, which can be redeemed for discounts, vouchers, or other incentives. The more responsible you are, the more rewards you get!

4. What happens to the collected waste?

Answer: After collecting the waste, we ensure proper segregation and recycling. Recyclable materials are sent to processing units, while non-recyclable waste is disposed of in an environmentally friendly manner. Our goal is to reduce landfill waste and promote sustainability.
